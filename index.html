FORESTRY TECH 

We are a forestry operation based in PNG that are developing timber tracking technology for small and medium scale operators. 

OPERATIONS MOBILE APP

Step 1: Order

The first step is we recieve an order for logs. Our boys from the village head out to select the logs to fufill the order.

Step 2: Select & Cut

In the forest the boys will select the tree's to cut and then cut them to order. Each log will be measured, tagged and recorded using our timber tracking mobile app.

Step 3: Process

The logs will then be processed at the logging site according to the order that was placed. Each batch of processed timber is recorded.

Step 4: Deliver

The logs will then be transported out of the forest to the person who ordered the logs. Each pick up of a batch of timber is recorded against the vehicle.
